Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This application ("we") respects and protects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

1. Account & Sign-in
The app is passwordless. On first use, it reads a device identifier (the Android advertising ID / GAID; if restricted or unavailable, a locally generated random id is used) to register and sign you in automatically — no phone number or email required.

2. Information We Process
· Device identifier: to recognize your account and attribute your creations.
· Content you upload: the finished-product photos and notes you add when checking in.
· Generation requests: the ingredients you pick and the recipes / posters generated.
· Usage statistics: anonymized, aggregated stats used to improve the service.

3. How We Use Information
· To provide recipe generation, poster generation, and creation recording/display.
· To generate images for you through an AI image model.

4. Third-party Services
Recipes and posters are produced through a third-party AI image-generation API; requests include only the necessary text description. We do not share your device identifier or your uploaded photos with it.

5. Local Storage
The app follows your system language; the local device identifier is stored on your device and can be cleared at any time.

6. Your Rights
You can reset the advertising identifier in system settings, or uninstall the app to clear local data. To delete your account or data, contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated; changes will be posted here.
